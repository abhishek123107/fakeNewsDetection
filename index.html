About Truth Lens

Learn about our technology and mission

Truth Lens is a machine learning web application that helps users detect fake news articles in real time. This project was created to combat the growing spread of misinformation in digital media.

How It Works

  • Trained on thousands of real and fake news articles
  • Uses TF-IDF Vectorization to convert text to numerical data
  • Utilizes Logistic Regression classifier for accurate prediction
